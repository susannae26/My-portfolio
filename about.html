About Me

My name is Susanna. I am a computer programmer and web designer. I enjoy traveling, reading, and spending time with my animals.

My journey in programming began by finding an online web course on HTML and CSS. I also downloaded a few different apps from which I learned some programming basics and techniques. My love, however, really blossomed when I found Python. I began taking an online course on Udemy by Dr. Angela Yu. She completely brought the language to life with coding projects and explanations that made the language easy to understand and enjoyable. After this, I can't seem to put my projects down and find myself lost hours later learning the ins and outs of python. My passion includes discovering the ways the programs I create can make my life and the lives of the people who use them easier and less wasteful.

As well as my passion for programming, I am an experienced photographer. In my travels to places like Paris, Barcelona, Rome, etc., I have had the pleasure of capturing a lot of the beauty they have to offer. I have a 5+ years experience in photoshop.
